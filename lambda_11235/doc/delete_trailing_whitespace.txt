                    Delete Trailing Whitespace	by Taran Lynn


Help on deleting trailing whitespace		*delete-trailing-whitespace*

1. Commands for deleting trailing whitespace		|dtw-commands|

==============================================================================
1. Commands for deleting trailing whitespace			*dtw-commands*

				*DeleteTrailingLines* *:DeleteTrailingLines*
:DeleteTrailingLines		Deletes all triling lines that only contain
				whitespace in the current buffer.

				*DeleteTrailingWhitespace* *:DeleteTrailingWhitespace*
:DeleteTrailingWhitespace	Deletes trailing whitespace from all lines in
				the selected range.
